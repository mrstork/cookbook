Lasagna

Amanda Ceniti

Serves 6 - 8

Time needed 90 min

Born in the heart of Italy lasagna is a staple in Italian cuisine. Treat your family to a hearty meal just like Nona used to for you.

Equipment

  • Caserole dish
  • Cheese grater

Ingredients

  • 48 oz crushed tomato
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 package of oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups grated mozerella
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 small onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Parsley, basil, oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Preperations

  1. Place ground beef in pan and season with salt and pepper. Cook until light brown.
  2. Add olive oil, onion, garlic
  3. Add tomato, herbs, stir, cook 15 min.
  4. Add the tomato sauce. Keep tasting and seasoning with herbs and spices to taste.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  6. Shred the entire ball of mozeralla
  7. In the casserole dish start making the lasagna layers with the pattern: sauce, noodles, sauce, cheese. Repeat until the dish is full or supplies run out. Put a little extra cheese on top
  8. Cover the top of the caserole dish in foil and bake for roughly 35 min
  9. If you like the top crispy, uncover the lasagna and bake for 10 more minutes or until cheese has a nice brown colour
