Lasagna

Born in the heart of Italy...

Time needed 90min

Serves 6-8

Equipment

  • Caserole dish
  • Cheese grater

Ingredients

48 oz
Crushed tomato
1 pound
Ground beef
Oven-ready lasagna noodles
2 cups
Grated mozerella
1/2 cup
Parmesan cheese
1
Small onion
5 cloves
Garlic
2 tablespoons
Olive oil
Parsley
Basil
Oregano
Salt
Pepper

Preperations

  • Shred the entire ball of mozeralla
  • Cook ground beef until brown
  • Olive oil, onion, garlic, add tomato, stir, herbs cook 15 min.
  • Add tomato sauce. Keep tasting.
  • In the casserole dish start making the lasagna layers with the pattern: sauce, noodles, sauce, cheese. Repeat until the dish is full or supplies run out. Note: Try to keep more cheese for the top layer
  • Cover the top of the caserole dish in foil and bake 375 degree for roughly 35 min
  • Uncover the lasagna and bake for 10 more minutes or until cheese is a nice brown colour.
